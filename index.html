Introduction

Hey, I'm Eric Laputka. I'm a game designer from the University of Connecticut. I am skilled in working in virtual reality, scripting, lighting, modeling, design, and leadership. I recently won an award for excellence in my most recent game, Pizza Time. I was the vice president of the game design club which allowed me to help and show others the joy of making games. Along with that I am currently working as a game designer at pyrebug Studios where I am able to work with a team to create enjoyable experiences. In addition to that I’ve co created a game in roblox with over 330 plays and a 100% like ratio.

Passions

My main passion is in game design. I loved playing games growing up and still love playing games. This is where my passion started and only grew from there. I also have a passion for 3d modeling, lighting, and texturing. I enjoy doing it but would consider that more as a hobby. I still have much to learn in game design, but I am passionate to keep learning and to grow as a game designer.

Goals

My goals are to develop my skills as a game designer and eventually get a job in the game design industry. However, I don’t just want to be good at game design, I want to be great! I want my talents to be recognized. I have much to learn and it looks like I have a mountain to climb in order to reach this goal, but I know my hard work and determination will get me there!
